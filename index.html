About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I am studying large-scale hypergraph learning for real-world applications with Prof. Hanghang Tong in IDEA Lab. Prior to joining UIUC, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Hanyang University and I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Hanyang University under the supervision of Prof. Sang-Wook Kim. My research interests mainly lie in large-scale data mining and machine learning on various types of data (e.g., graph, hypergraph, text, and image), with a special focus on discovering knowledge from real-world networks.
