Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I am working with Prof. Hanghang Tong. My research interests are in data mining and machine learning on large-scale real-world graph/hypergraph data. Prior to joining UIUC, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Hanyang University with Prof. Sang-Wook Kim. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Hanyang University, where I was advised by Prof. Sang-Wook Kim.
